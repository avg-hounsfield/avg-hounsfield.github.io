Mri T-Spine W/O Contrast

MRI Protocol Neuro

Indications

Indicated for pain in the mid-back, or symptoms of nerve compression at this level. Useful for evaluating disc herniations, fractures, or tumors.

Sequences

SequenceContrast
SAG T1Pre-contrast
SAG T2 HIGH RES.Pre-contrast
AX T2 (DISC SPACES)Pre-contrast
SAG STIRPre-contrast

Related Clinical Scenarios

Bitemporal hemianopia, sellar mass suspected, initial imaging
Brain tumor metastatic, screening, extracranial malignancy
Brain tumor on prior imaging, extra-axial suspected, pretreatment evaluation

Brain tumor primary, screening, genetic risk factors
