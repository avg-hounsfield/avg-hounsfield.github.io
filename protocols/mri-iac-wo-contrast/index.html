Mri Iac W/O Contrast

Protocol • Neuro

Indications

Used to evaluate symptoms like hearing loss, tinnitus, or vertigo. This exam focuses on the internal auditory canals and cranial nerves VII and VIII. For cholesteatoma evaluation, add DWI Propeller sequence.

Sequences

Sequence Contrast
AX FLAIRPre-contrast
AX 3D FIESTAPre-contrast

Related Clinical Scenarios

  • Tinnitus, nonpulsatile, bilateral, no hearing loss, no neurologic deficit, no trauma, initial imaging
  • Tinnitus, nonpulsatile, unilateral, no hearing loss, no neurologic deficit, no trauma, initial imaging
  • Vertigo, chronic, recurrent, unilateral hearing loss, initial imaging
  • Dizziness, nonspecific, without vertigo, ataxia, or other neuro deficits, initial imaging
  • Hearing loss, conductive, no mass in ear, initial exam
