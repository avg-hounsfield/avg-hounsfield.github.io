MRI Sacrum Without Contrast

MRI Protocol Neuro

Indications

For evaluation of pain in the sacrum or coccyx. Can be used to assess for stress fractures, tumors, or inflammatory conditions like sacroiliitis.

Sequences

SequenceContrast
AX, COR T1Pre-contrast
AX T2Pre-contrast
COR T2 STIRPre-contrast
SAG STIRPre-contrast
