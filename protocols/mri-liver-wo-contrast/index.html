Mri Liver W/O Contrast

MRI Protocol Abdomen

Indications

Detection and characterization of liver lesions, such as cysts, hemangiomas, or malignant tumors. Dynamic contrast helps differentiate between different types of lesions.

Sequences

SequenceContrast

AX T1Pre-contrast

COR T2Pre-contrast
AX T2 FSPre-contrast
AX DWIPre-contrast

Related Clinical Scenarios

Liver lesion, <1cm, incidental finding on noncontrast MRI, chronic liver disease
Liver lesion, <1cm, incidental finding on noncontrast or single phase CT, chronic liver disease
Liver lesion, <1cm, incidental finding on US, chronic liver disease
Liver lesion, <1cm, indeterminate on noncontrast MRI, hx of extrahepatic malignancy
Liver lesion, <1cm, indeterminate on noncontrast or single phase CT, hx of extrahepatic malignancy
